Health Tips & Prevention

🛡️ General Prevention Tips
  • Wash hands regularly with soap for 20 seconds
  • Drink clean water — boil or use filtered water
  • Use mosquito nets and repellents at home
  • Eat fresh food and avoid uncovered street food
  • Get vaccinated — check Ayushman Bharat scheme
  • Exercise daily and maintain healthy body weight
